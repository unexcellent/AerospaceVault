Defined as a [[Vector|vector]] for which holds: 
$$
<e_{i}, e_{j}> = \delta_{ij} = 
\begin{cases} 
1 \quad \text{if} \quad i = j \\
0 \quad \text{if} \quad i \neq j
\end{cases}
$$

It then holds:
$$
v = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n} v_{i} \cdot e_{i}, \quad \forall v \in \mathbb{R}^{n}
$$
