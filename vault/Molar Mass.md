#uni/courses/thermo1 

Symbol: $M$
Unit: $\frac{g}{mol}$

Molar mass is the ratio between the [[Mass]] and the [[Amount of Substance]] and is a constant for any substance.
$$
M = \frac{m}{n}
$$
-> $m$: [[Mass]]
-> $n$: [[Amount of Substance]]

# Common Substances

| Substance | Molar Mass in $kg/mol$ | Molar Mass in $g/mol$ |
| --------- | ---------------------- | --------------------- |
| Air       | $0.02896$              | $28.96$               |
