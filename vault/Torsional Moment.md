Symbol: $M_{T}$

Rotational equivalent of [[Moment]].

# For a Cylinder

$$
M_{T}(x) = G \cdot I_{T} \cdot \phi'(x)
$$
-> $G$: [[Shear Modulus]]
-> $I_{T}$: [[Torsional Moment of Inertia]]
-> $\phi$: [[Twist Angle]]
-> $x$: distance to the fix point of the cylinder along the rotation axis
