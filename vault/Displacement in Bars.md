When under [[Strain]], [[Mechanical Beams|bars]] change their length. 
![[Pasted image 20240211112807.png]]
$$
u(x) = \int_{0}^{x} \varepsilon(x) \ dx
$$
$$
u(x) = \frac{1}{EA} \int^{x}_{0} N(x) \ dx + \alpha_{T} \cdot \Delta T \cdot x + u_{0}
$$
-> $\varepsilon$: [[Strain]]
-> $N$: [[Stress Resultants#Normal Forces|Normal Force]]
-> $E$: [[Hookes Law|Young's Modulus]]
-> $A$: cross-sectional area
-> $\alpha$: temperature expansion coefficient
-> $\Delta T$: [[Temperature]] difference
-> $u_{0}$: initial displacement
