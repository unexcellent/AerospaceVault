#uni/courses/mech2 

Symbol: $I_{T}$

Quantity used to describe resistance to [[Deformation from Torsion|torsional deformation]].

# For a Cylinder

$$
I_{T} = \frac{\pi R^{4}}{2} = \frac{M_{T}}{G \cdot \phi'}
$$
-> $R$: total radius of the cylinder
-> $M_{T}$: [[Torsional Moment]]
-> $G$: [[Shear Modulus]]
-> $\phi$: [[Twist Angle]]
