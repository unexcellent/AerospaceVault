#uni/courses/materials 

A [[Primary Shaping]] method building up a structure by adding [[Material]] layer by layer.

# Advantages

- Lightweight Design 
- Highly complex parts at low cost 
- Functional integration 
- Mass customization and personalization at low cost

# Disadvantages

- Not suitable for mass production 
- Expensive system technology 
- Pre- and post- processing necessary 
- Lack of industry standards
