#uni/courses/thermo1 

Symbol: $An$
Unit: $J$

The anergy of [[Heat]] as well as all energy forms, is the part that is, at least in principle, **not** capable of doing [[Thermodynamic Work|work]] or being transformed into work.
$$
An_{q} = Q - Ex_{q}
$$
-> $Q$: [[Heat]] in the system
-> $Ex$: [[Exergy]]
