#uni/courses/electrical 

Describes the [[Electrical Energy]] stored in the [[Magnetic Field]].

If the [[Inductance]] is constant:
$$
W_{m} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot L \cdot I^{2} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \Phi \cdot \Theta
$$
-> $L$: [[Inductance]]
-> $I$: [[Current]]
-> $\Phi$: [[Magnetic Flux]]
-> $\Theta$: [[Magnetomotive Force]]
