#uni/courses/thermo1 

Symbol: $M$
Unit: $\frac{m}{s}$

The mach number is the ratio between the local flow velocity and the speed of sound.
$$
M = \frac{c}{\sqrt{\gamma \cdot R \cdot T}}
$$
-> $c$: local velocity
-> $\gamma$: [[Isentropic Exponent]]
-> $R$: [[Universal Gas Constant]]
-> $T$: [[Temperature]]
