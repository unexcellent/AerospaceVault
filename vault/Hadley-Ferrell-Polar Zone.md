The Hadley-Ferrell-Polar zones are major convection currents in [[Earth's Atmosphere]] that are caused by [[Hadley Cell|Hadley cells]] and the [[Coriolis Effect]].
![[Pasted image 20240825154446.png]]

These have a major correlation to precipitation levels at different latitudes.

- Low pressure zone at equator -> rain forests 
- High pressure zone at ~30° (horse latitudes) -> deserts 
- Low pressure zone at ~60° (polar front) 
- High pressure zone in polar areas -> „cold deserts“
